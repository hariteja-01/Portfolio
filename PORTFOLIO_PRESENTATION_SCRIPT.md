## 6) Professional Closing (10 to 15 seconds)

# Delivery Sheet (For Full Rubric Score)

## A) Posture and Body Language

- Walk to podium with calm pace and shoulders relaxed.
- Keep both feet stable, hip-width apart.
- Keep one hand lightly near podium, use the other for controlled gestures.
- Do not sway, rock, or lean repeatedly.
- Maintain a gentle smile at transitions.

## B) Eye Contact Pattern

- Start by looking at ma'am for first sentence.
- Then scan left center right every 4 to 5 seconds.
- On key metrics, look at ma'am while speaking the number.
- During project explanation, glance at screen briefly, then return to audience.

## C) Voice and Pace

- First line slow and clear.
- Use a small pause after each metric (for example, "9.18 CGPA" pause, "900 plus LeetCode" pause, "92 percent accuracy" pause).
- Keep speed moderate, not fast.
- End each section with a lower, confident tone.

## D) What Not To Do

- Do not read directly from screen.
- Do not use filler words repeatedly such as "like", "actually", "basically".
- Do not rush the project section.
- Do not turn your back fully to audience.

## E) Last 10-Second Impression

Use this exact final line:

"This portfolio is not just a collection of projects; it is my engineering journey in measurable outcomes. Thank you, ma'am."
